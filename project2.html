Status

Education is a tool to channelize population resource. Through proper guidance and trainning India can judiciously utilize its demographic dividend which is expected to peak by 2045. Through this initiative, we plan to provide a platform for guiding students in choosing appropiate academic streams and make them aware of global and national opportunities. Long term goal is to open a chain of schools which would just not be limited to holistic education but also provide India with efficient human work-force peculiar to its demand.

Aim

  1. Quality Education – lifelong learning and opportunities for all (SDG- 4)
  2. Gender Equality (SDG- 5)
  3. Skill demand match for decent work availability and economic growth (SDG- 8)
  4. Channelizing Demographic Dividend

Objectives

  1. Incorporating missing component in Quality Education and providing holistic training.
  2. Forecasting future job demands and preparing students at par with global standards.
  3. Exposing and disseminating information about variety of opportunities available abroad.
  4. Sensitizing them towards India’s need and guiding with relevant academic and professional choices.
  5. Industry-academia linkages at higher secondary level.

Conclusion

Children today will shape India's future tomorrow, if we don’t work in providing them proper guidance, we may find India in a demographic mess where population would be seen as problem rather than asset
